Membership and Access

Become part of the Chemical Society of Lesotho. Register as a student, lecturer, or industry member to access benefits and connect with the chemical community.

Membership categories

  • Student — access resources, events, and academic mentoring.
  • Lecturer — collaborate on workshops and curriculum development.
  • Industry Professional — connect with students and support internships.

Member advantages

  • Track your membership status and history
  • Receive event invitations and priority registration
  • Upload proof of payment securely
  • Maintain a professional profile in our community network
