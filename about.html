About the Chemical Society of Lesotho

We are a platform for chemistry lecturers, students, and professionals in Lesotho. Our mission is to build strong connections between academia and industry through collaboration, training, and shared resources.

Our purpose

We support undergraduate and postgraduate chemistry education, share professional development opportunities, and help researchers and employers discover each other. Our society is especially focused on practical training and applied spectroscopy, physical chemistry, and industrial chemistry.

Academic support

Provide study resources, lecture notes, and exam preparation guides for university chemistry students.

Industry partnership

Create internship pathways, project collaborations, and visits between companies and the National University of Lesotho.

Community events

Run workshops, guest lectures, and networking sessions where students can meet chemists from industry and research.

Who can join?

  • Chemistry students at NUL and other institutions
  • Lecturers, instructors, and academic staff
  • Professionals working in laboratories, manufacturing, and chemical services
  • Researchers, educators, and chemistry enthusiasts

What we provide

  • Member membership tracking and event registration
  • Resource library for spectroscopy and physical chemistry
  • Support for member research and funding proposals
  • Payment proof upload for secure membership processing
